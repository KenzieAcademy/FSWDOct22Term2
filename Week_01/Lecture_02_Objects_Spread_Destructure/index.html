Objects, and how to handle them

    In preparation for React, it is necessary that we gain a solid understanding two important data types:

    • Objects
    • Arrays

    It is important to be familiar with handling these data types (i.e. iterating through arrays, selecting elements at specific indexes, accessing the necessary properties of objects, etc.).

    Understanding some of the underlying characteristics of objects helps gain a better understanding of objects and arrays, so let's spend today's session looking into how Objects are handled in memory.
